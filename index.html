FAQ

How far in advance can I place my order?

Sep 22, 2020

Orders can be placed up to 4 weeks in advance.


Can I order catering the same day as my event?

Sep 30, 2020

Sadly no. For catering above 10 people, order at least two weeks in advance for adequate provisions to be made

How many will each catering serving item serve?

Aug 11, 2020

Each catering package will serve between 5-7 adults

Are plates/utensils included in my order?

Aug 20, 2020

Yes. Plates, serving utensils and cutlery are all included in your order

Can I reheat the food in a microwave?

August 29, 2020

Yes, the containers are microwave safe, however not oven safe. Food can also be held in the hot containers through chafing dish setups and stereo kits.

Is delivery available near me?

Sep 22, 2020

Unfortunately, we haven't established our delivery system. If an order is made early, we can come to a suitable arrangement for delivery.

If I have a party of 10, what should I order?

Sep 22, 2020

Two (2) family size packages.

How do I edit, change or cancel my order?

Sep 30, 2020

Call directly and speak to the manager or a representative.

Will cups and ice be served with my beverage?

August 15, 2020

Yes. 6 cups will be given for each gallon beverage bag. Ice is available upon request.

Who do I contact for additional questions?

Sep 22, 2020

Please contact us online through our website, send us an email or a whatsapp message. Our response time is quick.
